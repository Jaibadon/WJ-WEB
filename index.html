Whereʼs Jai, a fast-emerging, high-energy, pop rock band from Tāmaki Makaurau. Known for chaotic fun and emotionally charged shows.

Fronted by two dynamic powerhouse female singers Grace Allis and Leigh Edmeades, backed by Michael Walmsley (guitar), Jai Nielsen (bass), and Gene Todd (drums). Formed in 2021 and now all just 19, they have quickly made a name for themselves.
